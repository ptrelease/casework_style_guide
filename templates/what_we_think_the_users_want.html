What we think users want

  • Simple to use, doesn't need a manual
  • Easy to see and read
  • Responsive, fast, not flaky
  • Looks and feels relevant to the outside world. Will level up users in worldly web skills
  • Obvious to identify where you are and what you are doing
  • Gives a sense of ownership, because we've listened to and designed with users
  • Provides a feeling of doing a good job
